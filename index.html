About Me

Built on the
Mats, Forged
in Competition

I'm Rui Trovoada — a dedicated Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Gi-only practitioner with 5 years of training. My journey began on the mats of London and has taken me to competitions across Europe.

Specialising in guard passing and submission hunting, I bring a methodical, analytical approach to BJJ — studying footage, dissecting technique, and constantly evolving my game.

Off the mats, I coach beginners and intermediate practitioners, helping students develop their own unique style and build the confidence that only comes from consistent training and real sparring.

My Game

Styles &
Specialities

🛡️

Guard Passing

Pressure passing, knee slices, and torreando. I break guards systematically and control top position to set up submissions or secure points.

⚡

Submission Hunting

Triangle chokes and guillotines from everywhere — top, bottom, standing. I hunt finishes from every position with relentless intent.

🧠

Strategic Rolling

Methodical pace control. I study opponents, exploit weaknesses, and make tactical decisions that win on points when needed.

🥋

Gi Only

Pure Gi practitioner. I embrace the Gi fully — collar chokes, lapel games, and the technical chess match that makes Gi BJJ so rewarding.
