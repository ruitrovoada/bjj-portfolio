About Me

Built on the
Mats, Forged
in Competition

I'm Rui Trovoada — a dedicated Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu practitioner with 8 years of training across Gi and No-Gi disciplines. My journey began on the mats of London and has taken me to competitions across Europe and beyond.

Specialising in guard passing, leg locks, and submission hunting, I bring a methodical, analytical approach to BJJ — studying footage, dissecting technique, and constantly evolving my game.

Off the mats, I coach both beginners and advanced athletes, helping students develop their own unique style and build the confidence that only comes from consistent training and real sparring.

My Game

Styles &
Specialities

🛡️

Guard Passing

Pressure passing, knee slices, and tower passes. I break guards systematically and control top position to set up heavy ground-and-pound or submissions.

🔒

Leg Locks

Ashi garami specialist. Toe holds, knee bars, and heel hooks from outside positions. A dangerous finishing game from every angle.

⚡

Submission Hunting

Triangle chokes and guillotines from everywhere — top, bottom, standing. I hunt finishes from every position with relentless intent.

🧠

Strategic Rolling

Methodical pace control. I study opponents before matches, exploit weaknesses, and make tactical decisions that win on points when needed.

The Journey

My BJJ Path

2018

White Belt — First Roll

Walked into my first BJJ class in London. Tapped out six times in the first session. Never looked back.

2020

Blue Belt — First Gold

Won my first competition at London Open. 4 subs in 4 fights. Discovered a love for competition.

2022

Purple Belt — Going Full-Time

Promoted to purple belt and began training twice daily. Moved academies to train under world champion coaches.

2024

Brown Belt — World Champion

Won IBJJF World Championship at Brown Belt. First world title. Still hungry.

2026

Coaching & Expanding

Taking on private students, running seminars across Europe, and building this portfolio. The journey continues.
